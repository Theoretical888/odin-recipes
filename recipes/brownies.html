MMMMM... Brownies

Description:

These brownies with chocolate chips are the best brownies I've ever had!

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 ½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
